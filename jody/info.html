Instructions:
Tap the color wheel to initiate the first spin
Tap the color wheel again to stop the spin
Tap the "spin" icon to spin the wheel again
Tap the wheel to stop
Each time the wheel stops a new headline will appear on the screen
Each word can be repositioned individually or deleted via a tap
The words can also be dragged
This creates a word chain that follows the path of the drag
Letting go disperses the headlines creating an array of words that reads like concrete poetry
Tap the screen again to see the larger text and initiate a new spin
